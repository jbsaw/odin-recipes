Filipino Tapsilog

Description

A popular Filipino meal that combines tapa (beef), sinangag (garlic fried rice), and itlog (fried egg).

It is a very simple dish to make, just make sure your beef is sliced thin and your pan is hot!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 cup Calamansi (or lemon) Juice
  • 1 head Garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Pepper
  • 3 lbs Sirloin Steak, thinly sliced
  • Oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, calamansi juice, garlic, sugar, salt, and pepper. Stir until sugar and salt are dissolved. Add beef and massage to fully coat with marinade. Keep overnight in the refrigerator to cure. Drain meat from marinade.
  2. In a wide pan over medium heat, heat about 1 tbsp oil. Add beef in a single layer and cook, turning on sides, for about 3 to 5 minutes or until liquid is almost absorbed. Add another 1 tbsp oil and continue to cook until meat is lightly browned. Remove from pan and cut into serving portions. Serve hot.
